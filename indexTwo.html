WALKOFF

Automation for All

Is your environment suffering from...

Too many devices?

Too much data?

Repetitive manual processes?

WALKOFF can help

WALKOFF is an open source platform whose goal is to make integrating and automating components faster, cheaper, and easier than ever before.

The WALKOFF platform makes integrating across disparate devices, services, data, and capabilities easier than ever in a multitude of use cases including

  • Cybersecurity
  • Systems Administration
  • Analytics
  • Internet of Things
  • And more...

Allowing you to spend less time integrating and more time taking control of your environment.

WALKOFF is a Community Effort

WALKOFF is working together to build a community and create a common integration format so that we can spend less resources on capabilities that have already been built and more resources on accomplishing mission.

Anyone is welcome to build WALKOFF-enabled apps or contribute to the WALKOFF open source orchestrator. These apps can be deployed across any WALKOFF-enabled orchestrator or platform. Anyone is also welcome to make their products or platforms WALKOFF-enabled as well making it easier than ever to integrate with more, faster.
