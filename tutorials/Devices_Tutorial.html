Devices Tutorial

The fourth tab down in the sidebar on the left side of the page is the "Devices" tab, which allows you to manage the various apps and devices. Each app has one or more devices associated with it.
There are three different views on this page: the Apps view, the Devices view, and the Edit Device ("Parameters") view. The "Apps" panel shows a list of all the apps that have been loaded into WALKOFF. If you select an app, then the Devices panel below will show the device(s) associated with that app.
The Devices panel shows the devices that are associated with the highlighted app. You have the ability of adding a new device, removing a device, or editing a device. To add a new device, fill out the "Parameters" form on the right side of the page. You have the option of entering in the name of the device, a username and password of the device, as well as the IP Address and port of the device, if applicable. If no name is given, a default one will be provided. You may also select an already created device, edit it the same way you added a new device (i.e. via the "Paramaters" panel), and then click the "Edit Device" button once you are done to save your changes. Lastly, to remove a device from an app, click on the "Remove Device" button after selecting the device to be removed.
