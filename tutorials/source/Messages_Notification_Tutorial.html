Messages and Notifications

  • As workflows are executed, workflows may send users messages. When a new message arrives it will appear in your notifications drop-down menu. Notifications which are awaiting an action will have a red "!" beside them.

    Clicking on a notification will show the full message and mark it as read. The usernames of those users who have read the message will appear above the body of the message.

    To view all your messages, click the Messages tab

    Clicking on a message will display the full body of the message

    You can mark messages as read or unread by selecting multiple messages and selecting the letter icons. Note: Marking a message as unread does not mark it as unread to other users. Other users' display of this message will still display your username as having read the message.

    You can delete message from your inbox by selecting teh trash icon. Warning: This operation is unrecoverable.
