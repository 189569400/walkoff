Today's Cyber Analysts are Completely Overwhelmed

  • Attacks are happening constantly

  • There is not enough people or resources

  • And there is too much data to be able to confidently defend our networks


Help has started to emerge in the form of security automation with a class of devices called orchestrators. These devices automate the repetitive processes currently dragging your analysts down enabling you to refocus your resources on eliminating the cyber threat.

But this is not enough

  • Networks become Locked-In to these proprietary APIs

  • Development of orchestration capabilities are restricted to proprietary APIs and Data Structures

  • Identical capabilities are being developed repeatedly for each proprietary API


WALKOFF seeks to free Automation from the constraints of proprietary APIs By

  • Standardizing the way orchestration capabilities are developed

  • Fostering an active community across the Cybersecurity community and Commercial Industry

  • Creating an Open Source Implementation ensuring immediate impact


Join the Effort. Together We Can

  • Defend networks more efficiently and effectively

  • Make it cheaper and easier to create and deploy automated cyberdefense capabilities

  • Shape the future of cybersecurity
